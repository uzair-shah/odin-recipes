Baked Chicken Breast Recipe

Description

This is my favorite chicken breast recipe.

The chicken comes out juicy, tender and with a bit of spicy kick, so be warned!

Ingredients

Our very first dish will entail cooking a delcious chicken breast:

Things you will need

  • An airfried chicken breast
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 0.5 teaspoon salt
  • 0.5 teaspoon paprika
  • 0.5 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 0.5 teaspoon red pepper
  • 2 slices of a bread of your choosing
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1/4 iceberg lettuce

Steps

Steps to follow:

  1. Cut the chicken into slices
  2. Combine the sauces and powders in a small bowl to make the sauce
  3. Chop the cucumber into slices
  4. Chop up the lettuce
  5. Toast the bread and pour a slice of cheese on one of the slices
  6. Lather one side of the bread with the sauce mixture onces its done being toasted
  7. Add the cucumber and lettuce on top of the bread
  8. Place the chicken on top of the vegetables
  9. Place the other slice on top of the chicken

Voila!

Enjoy your Sammie.
