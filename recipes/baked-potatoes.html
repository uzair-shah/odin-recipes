Baked Potato Wedges Recipe

Description

Who doesn't love potatoes? Me, when I'm on a diet!

That's why I chose to use this baked potato recipe to get my freshly baked potatoes so that it soothes the soul (and the belly) guilt free

Ingredients

This dish entails pretty simple ingredients

Things you will need

  • 2 Potatoes (Medium sized each)
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Pepper to your taste
  • Salt to your taste
  • 0.5 teaspoon paprika
  • 0.5 teaspoon garlic powder

Steps

Steps to follow:

  1. Cut your potatoes into manageable pieces (Remember, the bigger they are the longer they take to cook)
  2. Combine the above ingredients in a small bowl and mix well
  3. Toss it in the air fryer which is set to 200 degrees Celcius
  4. Turn the potatotoes around after 10 minutes
  5. Cook for a further 8 minutes

Voila!

Enjoy your freshly baked potatoes. I wont tell anyone if you dont.
