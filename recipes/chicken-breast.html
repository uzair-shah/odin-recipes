Baked Chicken Breast Recipe

Description

This is my favorite chicken breast recipe.

The chicken comes out juicy, tender and with a bit of spicy kick, so be warned!

Ingredients

Our very first dish will entail cooking a delcious chicken breast:

Things you will need

  • 3-4 Chicken Breasts (170g each)
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 0.5 teaspoon salt
  • 0.5 teaspoon paprika
  • 0.5 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 0.5 teaspoon red pepper

Steps

Steps to follow:

  1. Thaw your chicken
  2. Combine the above items in a small bowl and mix well
  3. Let the chicken marinade in the mixture for atleast 45 minutes
  4. Toss it in the air fryer which is set to 200 degrees Celcius
  5. Turn the chicken around after 10 minutes
  6. Cook for a further 8 minutes

Voila!

Enjoy your freshly baked chicken
